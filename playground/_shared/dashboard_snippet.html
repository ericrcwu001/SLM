What this dashboard does and does not claim

✓ True today
  • Every output is a valid, safe-domain 17³ LUT by construction (grammar-constrained 64-code decode).
  • Deterministic, local, and cheap: fixed-seed greedy decode on a single T4, scale-to-zero.
  • Frozen tokenizer reconstructs held-out LUTs at mean ΔE00 1.31 / ~37 dB.
  • Router is production-ready: route accuracy 0.884, refuse & clarify recall 1.0.
◌ Not yet proven
  • No end-to-end supported_prompt_to_lut_pass_rate exists — the decode-dependent layers (L2, L4–L7) are still decoder-free proxies in the v1 spine.
  • Free-running greedy generation collapses (behavioral fidelity 0.159); best-of-N recovers to ≈0.42 against a 0.708 teacher-forced ceiling.
  • No headline quality gate has been met at scale, and no GRPO result is shipped.
